Angela's Cinnamon Roll Recipe

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • ¾ cup warm milk
  • 2 ¼ teaspoons yeast
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • ¼ cup butter
  • 3 cups bread flour


For the filling:

  • 2/3 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1 ½ tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • ¼ cup butter


Instructions:

  1. Mix the milk with the yeast and sugar in a bowl.
  2. Add the eggs and melted butter to the mixture.
  3. Incorporate the flour until a dough forms.
  4. Knead the dough for 10 minutes.
  5. Place the dough in a large bowl, cover with plastic wrap, and let it rise for 2 hours.
  6. After rising, roll out the dough into a rectangle.
  7. Prepare the filling by melting butter and mixing it with brown sugar and cinnamon.
  8. Spread the filling on the rolled-out dough and roll it into a Swiss roll.
  9. Cut the roll into 3 cm sections and place them flat in a baking tray.
  10. Preheat the oven to 350°F (180°C) and bake for 20-25 minutes until lightly browned.
