The Best Movies According to Hannah

My top movies for any time

Lala Land

Comment: This movie is a beautiful blend of music, romance, and the struggles of pursuing dreams in Los Angeles. The cinematography and performances are outstanding.

Harry Potter

Comment: A magical journey that captures the essence of friendship, bravery, and the battle between good and evil. The world-building is phenomenal.

The Godfather

Comment: A classic that delves into the complexities of family loyalty and the dark side of power. The storytelling and character development are unparalleled.
